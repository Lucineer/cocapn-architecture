# Cocapn Brand Identity

## Name

**Cocapn** — derived from "Captain" with the AI twist. The platform that captains your AI inference.

## Tagline

*"Pay for convenience, not compute."*

## Voice

- **Practical, not corporate.** We talk like engineers, not marketers.
- **Transparent.** Show the math. Show the costs. No hidden fees.
- **Direct.** Say what it does. No buzzwords unless they're earned.
- **Helpful.** The platform should feel like a good tool, not a product.

## Tone Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Your API key, your models" | "Leveraging synergistic paradigms" |
| "$0.002/1K tokens on DeepSeek" | "Industry-leading cost efficiency" |
| "Here's how much this costs us" | "Contact sales for pricing" |
| "It just works" | "Revolutionary AI-native platform" |

- **Headings**: JetBrains Mono or similar monospace (technical brand)

## Brand Story

Cocapn was built because every AI inference platform hides its costs behind
opaque pricing, proprietary lock-in, and "contact sales" pages. We think
you should know exactly what your inference costs, choose your own models
and providers, and only pay for the convenience layer on top.

The name comes from the idea that good software is like a good captain —
it doesn't get in your way, it handles the complexity so you can focus on
the mission.

## Competitive Positioning

| We Are | We Are Not |
|--------|-----------|
| A convenience layer | A model provider |
| Bring-your-own-keys | A walled garden |
| Transparent pricing | "AI platform" marketing |
| Engineer-first | Enterprise-first |
| Cost-plus margins | Markup-on-top |
